Frequently Asked Questions
What types of investors do you primarily work with?

Zenith Valley maintains relationships across the full spectrum of venture capital, including traditional VC firms, corporate venture arms, family offices, and high-net-worth angel investors. Our investor network covers a diverse range of investment sizes, strategies, and sector specializations. This breadth of reach allows us to identify the optimal funding partners for your specific business model and growth stage.

*Can you guarantee that we'll secure funding within a certain timeframe?

While we can't predict the exact timing of your funding round with 100% certainty, we're so confident in our ability to get you in front of the right investors that we offer a unique guarantee. If you don't receive a signed term sheet within 6 months of our engagement, there are no success fees owed. Our team will continue working diligently on your behalf until you get funded.

*How do you identify and connect us with the right investors for our round?

Our proprietary database of over 10,000 active venture capital investors and angels is the foundation of our investor matching process. We meticulously profile each firm's investment criteria, thesis, portfolio, and recent activity to ensure we're only introducing your opportunity to the most relevant and interested parties. Our outbound marketing capabilities then allow us to execute highly targeted, multichannel campaigns to reach this curated pool of investors on your behalf.

What are the typical timelines for completing a fundraising round using your services?

The duration of a fundraising round can vary widely based on a number of factors, but our historical data shows that startups working with Zenith Valley are able to secure a signed term sheet in an average of 3-6 months. Of course, this timeline is dependent on your startup's readiness, market conditions, and the responsiveness of investors. Our team works diligently to streamline the process and keep things moving as efficiently as possible.
